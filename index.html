About Me

My Journey

I started as a Research Associate right out of college, which gave me a strong analytical foundation. Over time, I realized I was drawn more to strategy and cross-functional problem-solving, so I intentionally explored adjacent fields before landing on Product Management — and it's been the right home ever since.

I began my product career in a UAT role deliberately chosen to build ground-up fluency in how engineering and product teams ship software. Within a year, I moved into an Associate PM role and grew into a full Product Manager, leading two teams across strategy, roadmap delivery, and cross-functional execution. To deepen my technical foundation and sharpen my ability to build data-driven products at scale, I pursued an MS in Business Analytics & Information Management at Purdue University.
